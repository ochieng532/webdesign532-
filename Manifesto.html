GREAT MINDS PARTY (GMP) MANIFESTO

“Inspiring Thought. Leading Change.”

1. Education for Innovation Increase access to quality and practical education.Promote innovation, research, and technical skills.Support free secondary education and subsidized tertiary education. Introduce smart learning in public schools.


2. Youth Empowerment Create job opportunities through digital economy and industrial growth.Fund youth entrepreneurship through grants and soft loans.Establish innovation hubs in all counties.


3. Economic Transformation Support small and medium enterprises (SMEs) as engines of growth.Lower the cost of living through responsible taxation and local production.Strengthen local manufacturing and agricultural value chains.


4. Technology & Digital Growth Expand internet access across rural and urban areas.Digitize government services for transparency and efficiency.Promote Kenya as a tech innovation hub in Africa.


5. Good Governance & Accountability Ensure zero tolerance to corruption.Open governance: Publish all public spending and projects.Devolve more funds to counties with strict auditing.


6. Healthcare for All Provide accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare.Upgrade public hospitals and support community health programs.Expand health insurance coverage for low-income families.


7. Climate Action & Sustainability Promote renewable energy adoption.Encourage tree planting and environmental conservation.Support green agriculture and water harvesting.


8. National Unity & Inclusion Embrace diversity and promote peace among all communities. Empower women, persons with disabilities, and marginalized groups.Promote fair representation at all levels of government.
